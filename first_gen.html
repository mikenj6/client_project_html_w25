First Gen Suport at UMSI

ABOUT US

The First-Generation Student Resource Center at the University of Michigan is dedicated to empowering students who are the first in their families to attend college. Our mission is to create an inclusive and supportive environment where first-generation students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We aim to address the unique challenges faced by first-gen students through tailored programs, resources, and mentorship. The center is staffed with individuals who understand the intricacies of navigating college life for the first time and are committed to helping students succeed. Learn more about our history, goals, and the passionate team behind our initiatives.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs specifically designed to support first-generation students throughout their college journey. Our orientation program prepares incoming students for the transition to campus life, covering everything from academic expectations to campus resources. Our mentorship program pairs first-gen students with faculty and staff members who provide guidance, support, and encouragement. Additionally, we offer workshops and seminars on topics like time management, financial literacy, career planning, and wellness. Our goal is to equip first-gen students with the tools and confidence they need to navigate their college experiences successfully.

Academic Support

First-gen students often encounter unique academic challenges, such as unfamiliarity with college-level coursework or a lack of awareness about available resources. Our center offers comprehensive academic support to help students excel in their studies. Tutoring services, study groups, and academic advising are available to ensure students have access to the guidance they need. We also offer workshops on effective study habits and test-taking strategies. Additionally, our staff can assist with course selection, major exploration, and understanding graduation requirements, providing a roadmap to academic success.

Financial Resources

Navigating the financial aspect of college can be daunting, but we are here to help. We provide information and assistance on a wide range of financial resources available to first-gen students. Whether you're seeking scholarships, grants, work-study opportunities, or financial aid counseling, our center can help you identify and apply for funding that aligns with your needs. We also offer workshops on budgeting, managing student loans, and financial planning for the future. Our goal is to alleviate financial stress so you can focus on your studies and personal growth.

Get Involved

Engaging with the campus community enriches the college experience, and we encourage first-gen students to get involved in various activities and initiatives. Whether it's joining student organizations, participating in workshops, or volunteering for community service projects, there are countless ways to make the most of your time on campus. Our center offers opportunities for students to contribute their perspectives, advocate for the first-gen community, and develop leadership skills. Learn how you can participate and make your mark at the University of Michigan.

Contact us

Whether you're a current student, prospective student, family member, or faculty interested in supporting first-gen students, we invite you to reach out to us. Our contact page provides phone numbers, email addresses, office hours, and location details. Our team is eager to answer questions, provide resources, and help you navigate your journey. We value open communication and are dedicated to assisting all members of the first-gen community.
